In JobHunterOP, offers are evaluated using a strict A-G scoring model to evaluate targets. Adjust the values on the right to see how the system ranks opportunities in real-time.

  • Compensation (30%): Base salary, equity multipliers, and local flexibility.
  • Growth & Team (20%): Engineering culture, runway, and funding signals.
  • Legitimacy (10%): Real posting versus headhunter spam or ghost role.
